Who am I?

Hey there! I'm AmbiguousPhoenix, but you can call me Luke. I'm a student working towards my Bachelor's degree in Creativity, with a particular interest in animation. Some of my other interests include game development, graphic design, photography, web development and going for walks in nature.

In my early tertiary days, I studied a bit of Information Technology, although I later decided it wasn't my thing, and I was more drawn to the creative, visual aspect of my current field of study.

Self-packaged stuff for ACES workflow with Maya, RenderMan and After Effects
The Academy Color Encoding System is meant to be a set of industry standard colour spaces for ensuring consistency throughout projects. Here I've packaged the ACEScg variant included with Pixar's RenderMan with the After Effects OpenColorIO plugin and some screenshots of the settings I used.

Microsoft PowerToys
This cool program adds a bunch of little features to Windows. I use it for remapping shortcuts, having a native color picker on demand and adding SVG and PDF previews to File Explorer.
